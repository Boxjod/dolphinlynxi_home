About Dolphin Lynxi

Embodied-AI data-asset operator · Data flows like tide

About Join us Contact

Dolphins are the smartest mammals in the ocean. They sense the world with sonar, survive through pod cooperation, and conquer the deep with agile grace.

Dolphin Lynxi takes its meaning from this: the future of embodied AI is not about who builds the coolest robot or scores highest on benchmarks — it's about who provides the most solid infrastructure for the entire industry.

We don't build robots.
We don't make chips.
We feed robots — and make "cooking the meal" simple, cheap, and sustainable.

Embodied AI is a long ocean expedition. Some build ships (the robots), some chart maps (the algorithms). We build the buoys, lighthouses and current-monitoring networks.

We don't chase the spotlight. But we know — without us, the whole fleet loses its way.
